Why Explore Space?

Our Solar System is home to breathtaking diversity — from the hot rocky world of Mercury, to the massive gas giant Jupiter, to the icy blue depths of Neptune.

Thanks to space missions and modern technology, we have visited the Moon, sent rovers to Mars, and explored distant worlds through powerful telescopes. GeoSphere invites you to journey beyond Earth and discover the remarkable system we call home.
