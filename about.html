About GeoSphere

Our home in space, the Solar System, consists of a beautiful collection of planets, moons, and asteroid belts which revolve around its one and only star — the Sun. Each planet has its unique story, whether it be from the hot rocky world of Mercury to the massive icy gas giant Neptune. Together, they form a system that has fascinated humanity for centuries.

What makes the Solar System exciting is not just its structure, but the sense of exploration it inspires. For generations, people have looked up at the night sky and wondered what lies beyond. Thanks to space missions and modern technology, we have been able to visit the Moon, send rovers to Mars, and explore distant planets through powerful telescopes and satellites.

Our Mission

GeoSphere is dedicated to exploring the wonders of our Solar System in a simple and engaging way. It provides an overview of the planets, dwarf planets, and important space missions that have helped us better understand our cosmic surroundings.

Whether you are a curious learner or simply fascinated by space, GeoSphere invites you to take a journey beyond Earth and discover the remarkable system we call home. Knowledge of the cosmos connects us all — gazing at the same stars, sharing the same Sun, living on this remarkable pale blue dot we call Earth.

GeoSphere is a student front-end project built with HTML and CSS,
designed to make space accessible and engaging for everyone.
